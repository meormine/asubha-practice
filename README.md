A minimalist, distraction-free digital environment dedicated to the contemplation of impermanence, body mindfulness, and the nature of decay.

---

## Purpose of the Practice

**Asubha meditation** is a traditional mindfulness practice centered on contemplating the unattractive, transient, and impure nature of physical form. This application serves as a modern tool to support this discipline, aiming to achieve specific meditative goals:

* **Countering Attachment:** Cultivating a realistic view of the body to diminish sensory desire, vanity, and infatuation with physical appearance.
* **Reflecting on Impermanence:** Facing the reality of aging, illness, and mortality to foster a deeper sense of urgency and diligence in daily mindfulness.
* **Developing Equanimity:** Breaking through superficial perceptions of beauty to cultivate a grounded, peaceful, and balanced mind.

By presenting a random sequence of reflective texts paired with corresponding imagery, the tool prevents the mind from becoming desensitized, ensuring each contemplation session remains fresh and impactful.

---

## Design Philosophy

The application is deliberately stripped of complex interfaces, metrics, or notifications to preserve the sanctity of the practice:

* **Quiet Focus:** No social feeds or tracking. The environment is designed entirely to facilitate deep, solitary reflection.
* **Contemplative Themes:** Features a **Dark Mode** for low-light reflection and a soft **Paper Mode** (cream background with charcoal text) that mimics the comforting, non-glare experience of reading a classic book.
* **Intentional Examination:** The fullscreen view includes a high-attenuation zoom feature, allowing closer, deliberate examination of the imagery to deepen the visualization practice.

---

## Core Features

* **Cycle Meditation:** A localized randomization system ensures that every text and image in the collection is presented exactly once before any item repeats, offering a complete and varied practice circuit.
* **Personal Library:** A built-in favorite system allows the saving of specific text-and-image combinations that resonate deeply, creating a personalized cycle for focused repetition.
* **Seamless Navigation:** Designed for effortless pacing. A single click, tap, or press of the `Spacebar` transitions to the next object of reflection.
* **Sharing Tools:** Includes clean formatting options to copy passages directly for offline study or journaling, alongside an integrated RSS feed for updates.

---

## How to Use

1. **Next Reflection:** Click anywhere on the background, use the arrow button (`→`) in the top panel, or press the `Spacebar` to load a new card.
2. **Deep Study:** Click directly on any image to open the fullscreen view. Use a mouse wheel, double-click, or pinch-to-zoom on mobile to focus on details.
3. **Save for Later:** Click the star icon on an active card to add it to a personal collection. Toggle the star icon in the top navigation bar to switch between the general pool and saved favorites.
